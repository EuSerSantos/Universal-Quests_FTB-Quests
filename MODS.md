### 📌 How to Install & Language Notice:
1. Download the `.snbt` file of the mod you want from the `chapters/` folder.
2. Place it inside your modpack's directory at: `.minecraft/config/ftbquests/quests/chapters/`
3. Restart your game or run `/ftbquests reload` in-game!

⚠️ **Language Notice:** Some quest chapters might be written in different languages depending on their original source. If you need automatic translation inside your game, we highly recommend installing the [FTB Quests Translator](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/ftb-quests-translator) mod!
